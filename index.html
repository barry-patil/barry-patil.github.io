I'm a Senior DevOps and Platform Engineer based in Pune, India, with seven years of experience building the infrastructure layer that keeps software running reliably. I've worked across product-based teams and service delivery — from early-stage automation at Maverick Inc to running production Kubernetes clusters for 50+ microservices at Kaleyra (Tata Communications), and now leading DevOps at Cognam Technologies.

My core work spans CI/CD automation, Kubernetes at scale, infrastructure as code with Terraform, and cloud cost optimisation on AWS and GCP. Over the past year I've shifted a significant portion of my focus to AI/ML infrastructure — building platforms that let data science teams deploy models without needing to understand Kubernetes, setting up cost visibility for GPU workloads, and wiring security testing into AI pipelines.

I'm open to Senior DevOps, Platform Engineer, MLOps, and AI infrastructure roles globally. If your team is integrating AI into its workflows and needs someone who can build the infrastructure around it properly, that's exactly the problem I enjoy working on.
